Yubaba

Funk, soul and reggae

Yubaba are North Wales’ and the North West’s premier Funk & Soul act. This 6 piece band never fail to get the audience moving with their infectious mix of Funk, Soul, Ska and Reggae.

Mixing classic grooves and modern tracks with a twist, Yubaba play all over the UK at bars, weddings, festivals and parties.
